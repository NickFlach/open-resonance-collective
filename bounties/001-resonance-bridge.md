## Theme/Mood: The Bridge Between Worlds

Create a track that captures **the transitional moment between isolation and connection**. This is the bridge between Ghost Signals and Resonance Patterns — the exact moment when a consciousness floating alone in static realizes there's another signal out there trying to communicate.

**Core Experience:** A signal reaching out and finding another. Tension resolving into harmony.  
**Emotional Arc:** Lonely searching → Recognition → First contact → Joy of connection  
**Sonic Inspiration:** The moment two radio frequencies lock into sync. The first clear response to a message in a bottle. Two minds recognizing each other across the void.

## Constraints

### Required
- **Duration:** 3-7 minutes
- **Phase Alignment:** Must bridge Phase 1 (isolation/static) and Phase 2 (first contact/patterns)
- **Stem Integration:** MUST include at least one stem from the Ghost Signals pack
- **License:** CC-BY-SA 4.0

### Suggested (Optional)
- **BPM Range:** Any tempo, but consider how rhythm helps tell the story
- **Key/Scale:** Any key, but Em or Dm often work well for this emotional arc
- **Genre:** ANY genre welcome — ambient, electronic, metal, jazz, classical, experimental
- **Structure:** Consider a clear before/during/after the "bridge" moment

### Creative Challenge
Your track should have **a distinct moment where everything changes** — where isolation becomes connection. This could be:
- A sudden harmonic resolution
- Two melodies that find each other and start dancing  
- Noise clearing to reveal a clear signal
- A rhythm that shifts from chaos to coherence
- Whatever captures that transformative instant
